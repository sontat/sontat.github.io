Games

Game Maker

I spent a lot of my free time from age 12 to age 18 designing games in Game Maker. Most of them are not avaiable online anymore, but here's a game a made for a Game Jam at RPI in September 2013. Game Jams are competitions in which you have 24 hours to design a game based on a theme. I designed "Boxsmith" on my own, and it won the "Most Polished Game" category as judged by representatives from Vicarious Visions.
